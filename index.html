Teach a network to distinguish divisibillity by d

  • u'ji = uji + η * Ii * δZi
  • v'j = vj + η * Zi * δO

where

  • δZi = g'(Zj) * vj * δO
  • δO = g'(O) * (Y - O)
  • g'(x) = g(x) * (1 - g(x))
